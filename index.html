About Me

Sana Ebrahimi is a Computer Science (CS) senior at Sharif University of Technology. For her thesis, she is conducting research in the field of semidefinite programming as a joint project under the supervision of Professor Nezam Mahdavi-Amiri and Andreas Karrenbauer. Last summer, She was a summer fellow under the supervision of Dr. Adish Singla in the Machine Teaching Group, Max Planck Institute of Software System. Sana aims to pursue a PhD in CS starting Fall 2019. For more information about me please see my resume here.
